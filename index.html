About Me

I am a physicist and computer science student with a strong interest in network and cloud technologies. I enjoy exploring complex systems.

I am also enthusiastic about my homelab, where I experiment with Linux, server administration, and automation tools with great focus on multimedia processing and (self-hosted) LLM experiments. It’s my personal playground for learning and testing new technologies, and I plan to share more about these projects here soon.

In the future, I will also write about my other projects and hobbies.

I invite you to check my projects at github.com/jacekstrzalkowski and also my old projects github.com/jacawaca, which were mostly done during Applied Physics program.
